Welcome to JS8Call-Improved

JS8Call-Improved is a community-driven evolution of JS8Call, bringing modern features, active development, and long-term support to HF digital communication. It’s fully compatible with existing JS8Call versions
(2.2.0, 2.2.1-devel and 2.3.1) while adding new capabilities and refinements.
